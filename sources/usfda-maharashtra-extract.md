## Notes on Data

- Registration status can be "Active", "Expired", or "Pending" — only Active registrations were retained.
- FEI (Firm Establishment Identifier) number is the unique ID for each establishment.
- Firms with multiple sites appear more than once; duplicates were collapsed to unique company names.
